A crucial component of cybersecurity is vulnerability scanning. This process involves systematically examining a network or system to identify and assess weaknesses that could be exploited by malicious actors. Vulnerability scanners are tools designed to detect vulnerabilities within a network, software, or hardware. They work by comparing the details of the target against a database of known vulnerabilities and producing a report that highlights potential risks.

Vulnerability scanning helps organizations proactively identify and address security flaws before they can be exploited. By mitigating these vulnerabilities, organizations can significantly reduce their risk of cyberattacks and enhance their overall security posture.
